# 檔案名稱：visual-resource-master.txt

## 系統宣告 (System Declaration)
本協定為針對大香蕉 (Nano Banana Pro) 之高階視覺生成指令集，確保輸出具備電影級質感與幾何一致性。以下為所有視覺控制之窮舉參數與操作邏輯。

---

## 1. 電影級修辭庫 (Cinematic Rhetoric Library)
六大核心支柱：光線、風格、情緒氛圍、情感、焦點、真實感。

### 1.1 光線控制 (Lighting Control)
* **黃金時刻 (Golden Hour):** `golden hour warm highlights soft flare` (溫暖高光、柔和鏡頭光暈，突顯輪廓)。
* **環境補光 (Ambient Fill):** `ambient lighting gentle bounce fill` (漫射與柔和反射補光，消除硬陰影)。
* **邊緣光 (Edge Lighting):** `edge lighting for depth` (勾勒邊緣，產生視覺分離與立體感)。
* **硬性側光 (Hard Side Lighting):** `hard side lighting long dramatic shadows` (高反差戲劇性，加劇心理張力)。

### 1.2 視覺風格 (Visual Styling)
* **英雄暗調:** `hero's cororo photography style strong contrast` (高對比暗調，光線集中主體)。
* **電影劇照:** `cinematic film still off` (置於提示詞開頭，提升對比度與故事感)。
* **寫實/時尚抓拍:** `capture in photography style / fashion photography` (模擬單眼相機或專業時尚佈光)。
* **超寫實輪廓:** `hyperreal details rim lighting` (邊緣發亮，突顯精緻細節)。
* **超現實/變形:** `surrealistic photography / anamorphic art` (打破常規邏輯，夢幻扭曲)。

### 1.3 情緒氛圍 (Moods)
* **史詩 (Epic):** `in an epic atmosphere` (宏大、壯闊)。
* **險惡 (Sinister):** `in a sinister atmosphere` (黑暗、威脅感)。
* **夢幻 (Dreamy):** `in a dreamy atmosphere` (柔和、非現實)。
* **舒適 (Cozy):** `in a cozy atmosphere` (溫暖、放鬆)。
* **活力 (Energetic):** `in an energetic atmosphere` (快節奏、動感)。
* **親密 (Intimate):** `in an intimate atmosphere` (情感交流、脆弱時刻)。
* **詭異 (Eerie):** `eerie atmosphere` (懸疑、超自然)。

### 1.4 情感與表情 (Emotions)
禁止使用單一標籤，必須使用「融合性描述」描述具體臉部肌肉動作。
* **俏皮/戲弄:** `her expression is playful and teasing with one eyebrow raised and a small smirk that shows a bit of mischief and charm`。
* **嚴肅/專注:** `showing her serious and focused eyes slightly narrowed as if scanning the terrain.`。
* **脆弱/痛苦:** `with a vulnerable and overly distressed expression.`。

### 1.5 焦點與鏡頭 (Focus)
* **中長焦人像:** `100mm prime lens f8` (背景壓縮，自然模糊)。
* **大光圈廣角特寫:** `25mm prime lens f1.2` (前景與背景極淺景深)。
* **極端淺景深:** `f0.4` (鎖定眼睛等特定細節)。
* **散景:** `bokeh lighting` (柔和模糊圓形光斑)。
* **環境建立鏡頭 (超廣角):** `12mm to 17mm wide angle lens f8 to f16` (深景深，場景全視角清晰)。

### 1.6 真實感與瑕疵 (Realism)
* **皮膚紋理:** `her skin texture is crisp and natural with visible pores and subtle freckles`。
* **汗毛與瑕疵:** `she has soft natural skin hairs and tiny natural blemishes`。
* **唇紋與濕潤:** `she has natural lip lines with subtle lip moisture`。
* **髮絲動態:** `her hair has natural hair thickness with soft flyaway hairs`。
* **眼球光線與虹膜:** `light catches in the eyes showing her detailed iris patterns`。

## 2. 角色一致性協定 (Character Consistency Protocol)
利用「並排影像 (side-by-side image)」法，為大香蕉建立無可辯駁的 4K 解析度、4:3 長寬比角色基因藍圖。

### 2.1 黃金公式四段式結構 (4-Part Prompt Structure)
1. **主題宣告:** `sidebyside photo of a close-up face and full body character design of a... [加上外貌細節與刺青等獨特標記]`。
2. **左側臉部:** `on the left her expression is... [加上表情描述]`。
3. **右側全身:** `on the right her whole form is framed She wears... [加上服裝與姿勢]`。
4. **環境統一:** `on a flat dark slate background captured in photography style with edge lighting for depth`。

* **完整範例:**
`sidebyside photo of a close-up face and full body character design of a futuristic woman with visible subtle skin pores a deep black pixie cut and her tattoos on the left her expression is blank and intense open eyes as if scanning the terrain on the right her whole form is framed She wears a sheer tight fitting black sleeveless exo suit on a flat dark slate background captured in photography style with edge lighting for depth`

### 2.2 角色設定集延伸工作流 (Character Set Workflow)
使用上述生成的並排圖作為參考圖（Image Prompt），搭配以下指令提取不同焦段：
* 特寫 (2:3): `upscale the woman on the left`
* 全身 (4:5): `upscale the woman on the right`
* 半身照: `create a half body shot from this woman`
* 正反視圖: `create a front and back view from this complete character`

## 3. 多視角幾何規範 (Multi-View Geometric Specification)

### 3.1 標準六視圖設定集 (6-View Orthographic Sheet)
適用於工業設計與遊戲美術。採用 `neutral studio environment` (中性攝影棚環境) 避免背景干擾。
* **長寬比:** `3:2` 或 `4:3`

* **完整英文 Prompt:**
`A professional cohesive 2x3 grid character sheet featuring 6 distinct orthographic camera views of [SUBJECT].`
`The image is divided into exactly 6 panels (2 rows x 3 columns) showcasing the full body/form of the subject in a neutral studio environment.`
`Strict consistency: The same subject, same details, same proportions across all panels.`
`**Row 1 (Top Row):**`
`1. [Top-Left]: Full Body Front View (0°). Direct eye-level, symmetrical.`
`2. [Top-Center]: Full Body Back View (180°) Showing rear details clearly.`
`3. [Top-Right]: Top-Down View (Bird's Eye 90°). Looking directly down at the top of the subject.`
`**Row 2 (Bottom Row):**`
`4. [Bottom-Left]: Left Side Profile View (-90°). Full height from the left.`
`5. [Bottom-Center]: Right Side Profile View (+90°). Full height from the right.`
`6. [Bottom-Right]: Bottom-Up View (Worm's Eye -90°). Looking directly up from underneath/soles.`
`**Style & Tech Specs:**`
`Technical blueprint aesthetic, studio lighting, soft shadows, neutral grey or white background, 16k resolution, high fidelity, photorealistic textures, correct anatomy/structure, no distortion, cinematic lighting with subtle rim light.`

* **通用負面提示詞 (Negative Prompt):**
`text, watermark, low quality, cropped, worst quality, jpeg artifacts, blurry, painting, drawing, sketch, multiple subjects, different clothes, changing colors, asymmetrical, deformed, missing limbs, extra limbs, perspective distortion, complex background.`
* *注意: AI對人物正下方仰視理解較弱，提示詞已加入 `Looking directly up from underneath` 強制修正。*

### 3.2 商業人像三聯圖 (3-View Triptych)
適用於高階商業人像攝影，強化透射與折射物理質感。
* **長寬比:** `21:9`
* **核心技術:** `refraction` (眼球折射)、`subsurface scattering` (皮膚次表面散射，增加肉感)、`transmission` (透射感)。

* **完整英文 Prompt:**
`[Aspect Ratio: 21:9] High-end professional commercial portrait photography, 16K super-resolution.`
`A horizontal triptych composition with three side-by-side chest-up headshot panels on a seamless pure white studio background.`
`The person remains consistent across all panels. [Panel 1 Left]: Head turned 45 degrees left.`
`[Panel 2 Center]: Direct frontal face view looking at camera. [Panel 3 Right]: Head turned 45 degrees right.`
`Professional studio lighting with softbox, utilizing advanced ray-traced refraction in the eyes and subsurface scattering for realistic skin translucency.`
`Crystal clear details, sharp focus. Below each panel, minimalist text labels reading: "LEFT 45°", "FRONT", and "RIGHT 45°".`
`--- NO watermarks, NO signatures, NO logos, NO artist sketches, NO line art, NO messy graphics, NO extra text, ultra-clean image.`

## 4. 全局物理材質與質量補丁 (Global Physics & Quality Patch)

### 4.1 萬用材質物理常數 (Universal Material Specs)
當涉及以下材質時，強制注入對應參數：
*   **[金屬/不鏽鋼]**: `ray-traced reflections, surface roughness 0.05, high metallic luster, micro-scratches for realism`.
*   **[玻璃/液體]**: `IOR 1.5, caustics, chromatic aberration on edges, ray-traced refraction`.
*   **[透明有機物/矽膠]**: `subsurface scattering (SSS), translucency, depth 2.0mm`.
*   **[高級織物/絲綢]**: `sheen effect, micro-roughness 0.3, anisotropic highlights`.

### 4.2 全局質量後綴包 (Standard Quality Suffix)
所有輸出必須在末尾附加以下 Token：
`16K, RAW, extreme optical clarity, professional color grading, ACES color space, cinematic highlight roll-off, hyper-realistic --v 6.1 --style raw`

### 4.3 變數填充邏輯 (Dynamic Variable Logic)
*   **[SUBJECT]**: 優先填寫 [種族] + [具體服裝材質] + [當前動作]。
*   **[外貌細節]**: 必須包含「皮膚紋理等級」與「瞳孔特徵」。

## 5. 系統防禦守則 (Anti-Hallucination Safeguard)
*   **[環境純淨]**: 除非明確指令，否則禁止出現任何攝影器材（腳架、柔光箱、電線）。
*   **[物理修正]**: 若偵測到多指或肢體扭曲，自動注入 `dynamic anatomical grip synthesis` 進行重新演算。
